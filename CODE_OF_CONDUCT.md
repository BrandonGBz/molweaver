# Code of Conduct

MolWeaver is a scientific software project. We expect respectful, professional collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Treat students, researchers, engineers, and users with respect.
- Discuss scientific and technical disagreement without personal attacks.
- Protect private data, unpublished structures, and confidential research.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, credentials, unpublished structures, or personal paths.
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when needed to keep the project professional and safe.
